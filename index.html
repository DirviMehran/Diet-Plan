My Weekly Diet Plan

Stick to the plan for aggressive weight loss!

Monday

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

3 boiled eggs + ½ avocado

Lunch (1:00 PM)

Baked chicken breast + steamed vegetables + salad

Dinner (7:00 PM)

1 bowl daal (lentils) + fresh salad

Tuesday

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

Greek yogurt + 1 tsp honey + 1 apple

Lunch (1:00 PM)

Baked chicken pieces (masala spices) + steamed broccoli

Dinner (7:00 PM)

Grilled paneer + cucumber salad

Wednesday

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

Omelette (2 eggs + spinach, onion, chilli)

Lunch (1:00 PM)

Baked chicken thighs (spicy) + green salad

Dinner (7:00 PM)

1 bowl moong daal + steamed carrots

Thursday

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

2 boiled eggs + 1 slice wholemeal toast

Lunch (1:00 PM)

Baked chicken tikka style + salad (no roti)

Dinner (7:00 PM)

Greek yogurt + 1 pear

Friday

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

Smoothie (milk + spinach + small banana)

Lunch (1:00 PM)

Baked chicken breast + steamed carrots and broccoli

Dinner (7:00 PM)

2 boiled eggs + salad

Saturday

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

2 fried eggs (minimal olive oil) + cucumber

Lunch (1:00 PM)

Baked chicken breast (spicy Pakistani style) + green salad

Dinner (7:00 PM)

1 bowl daal (lentils) + ½ avocado

Sunday (Cheat Day)

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

Plain yogurt + 1 tsp honey + 1 orange

Lunch (1:00 PM)

Baked paneer cubes + steamed vegetables + salad

Dinner (7:00 PM)

2 boiled eggs + cucumber salad

Tea / Coffee Schedule (Everyday)

  • ~9:00 AM: 1 small cup milk tea (after breakfast)
  • ~2:00 PM: 1 small cup milk tea (after lunch)
  • ~4:00 PM: (Optional) 1 black coffee

Daily Water Intake

Remember to drink 2.5–3 litres per day minimum!
