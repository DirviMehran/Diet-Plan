Suggested Weekly Grocery List

This is an approximate list based on the diet plan. Adjust quantities based on your specific needs and portion sizes.

Proteins

  • Eggs (Approx. 15-17)
  • Chicken Breast (Approx. 4-5 portions)
  • Chicken Thighs (1 portion)
  • Paneer (Approx. 2 portions)
  • Daal (Lentils - Moong or mixed)

Vegetables

  • Avocado (Approx. 2)
  • Mixed Salad Greens (Lettuce, etc.)
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Green Chilli
  • Cucumber
  • Other Steamed Vegetables (Your choice - e.g., green beans, cauliflower)

Fruits

  • Apples (1)
  • Pears (1)
  • Oranges (1)
  • Banana (1 small)

Dairy & Other

  • Greek Yogurt / Plain Yogurt
  • Milk (for smoothie & tea)
  • Honey (small amount)
  • Wholemeal Toast (1 slice)
  • Olive Oil (minimal for frying)

Pantry & Spices

  • Masala Spices (for chicken)
  • Tikka Style Spices (for chicken)
  • Pakistani Style Spices (for chicken)
  • Salt, Pepper
  • Tea bags/leaves
  • Coffee (optional)
